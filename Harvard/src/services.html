Law Services

We assist students of all ages in the area of school discipline, including suspensions, expulsions, and violations of student rights. The education lawyers at KCL, Andra Hutchins and Milt Kerstein have successfully represented students who have been wrongfully expelled, excluded or dismissed from their schools or colleges, as well as those who wish to challenge a suspension or other disciplinary action.
